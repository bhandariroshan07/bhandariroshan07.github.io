About Me

Who I Am

I am a Geomatics Engineer with hands-on experience in UAV-based data acquisition, LiDAR processing, and geospatial analytics — motivated to advance research integrating machine learning with high-resolution remote sensing.

I have contributed to UAV mapping, LiDAR feature extraction, bathymetric surveying, flood-risk assessment, and multi-sensor spatial data integration for environmental and infrastructure applications across Nepal.

My research interests centre on integrating GIS, multi-sensor remote sensing (LiDAR, SAR, hyperspectral, multispectral), and GeoAI to develop machine learning–based methods for UAV- and LiDAR-derived spatial analysis, 3D terrain characterisation, and data-driven hazard and environmental assessment.
